Stallion

A stallion is a male horse who is capable of reproduction.

  • Scientific Name:
    • Equus
  • Average Length:
    • 8 feet
  • Average Lifespan:
    • 27 years
  • Habitat:
    • Grasslands

Stallions are commonly known for having thick, cresty necks and well-muscled bodies. These characteristics are often distinct in stallions. Stallions are often known to be hard to work with at times, but with the right training and care they can be easier to handle.
